## 📌 Project Overview

The **Hospital Revenue & Profitability Analytics Dashboard** is an end-to-end Business Intelligence solution developed in **Power BI** to provide comprehensive insights into hospital financial performance, patient demographics, operational efficiency, insurance coverage, and departmental performance.

This dashboard transforms healthcare data into actionable insights, helping hospital administrators, healthcare managers, and decision-makers optimize resources, improve profitability, and enhance operational performance.

---

## 🎯 Business Objective

Healthcare organizations generate large volumes of operational and financial data. The objective of this project is to:

- Monitor revenue and profitability
- Analyze patient demographics and trends
- Evaluate insurance coverage performance
- Track departmental efficiency
- Monitor operational and medication costs
- Support data-driven healthcare decisions
